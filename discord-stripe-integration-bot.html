Common Stripe + Discord workflows

  • SaaS access gating: customer subscribes via Stripe checkout, receives "Premium" role, gains access to private channels and bot commands
  • Course/community paywall: one-time purchase unlocks lifetime access to educational channels and materials
  • Tiered membership: Bronze/Silver/Gold pricing tiers map to different Discord roles with escalating perks
  • Churn prevention: bot detects failed payments, DMs the member with a payment update link, alerts staff if unresolved after 48h
  • Revenue alerts: post to a staff channel when a new sale, refund, or dispute occurs with full transaction details
