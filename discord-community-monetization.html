Monetization features we build

Every community has different revenue potential. We build the exact combination that fits yours.

Paid role subscriptions

Members pay monthly for premium roles via Stripe checkout. Automatic assignment on purchase, automatic revocation on cancellation. Tiered pricing (Bronze, Silver, Gold) with escalating channel access and bot perks.

Premium content gating

Lock specific channels, categories, voice rooms, or bot commands behind active payments. Members see what they are missing, creating natural upgrade pressure. Works with one-time purchases or recurring subscriptions.

Token economies

Virtual currency systems where members earn tokens through activity, quests, and engagement. Tokens are redeemable for roles, perks, raffle entries, or real-money store items. Configurable inflation controls and earning curves.

Digital storefronts

In-Discord shops where members purchase digital goods: exclusive content, custom roles, server cosmetics, one-on-one sessions, or downloadable files. Stripe-powered checkout with automatic delivery.

Tipping and donations

Members tip creators, staff, or other members directly inside Discord. Configurable minimum amounts, leaderboards for top supporters, and thank-you automations. Integrates with Stripe or PayPal.

Monetizable games

In-Discord games that generate revenue directly. Poker with real-currency buy-ins, slot machines, gacha pulls, trivia with entry fees, raffle systems, and loot crate mechanics. All with configurable house edges, payout tables, and responsible spending limits.

Revenue analytics

Track MRR, churn rate, LTV, conversion funnels, and revenue per member. Daily or weekly summaries posted to a staff channel. Web dashboards for deeper analysis and CSV exports.

  • Education and course servers: paid course access gated by channel, drip-released content modules, cohort enrollment with automatic expiry, certificate generation on completion

  • Trading and alpha groups: paid signal channels with Stripe subscriptions, trial periods with automatic conversion, referral programs with commission tracking, churn prevention workflows
  • NFT and Web3 communities: token-gated channels based on wallet holdings, holder verification, snapshot-based airdrops, secondary market price alerts

The revenue stack: how each layer compounds

Effective community monetization is not a single feature. It is a system where each layer reinforces the others.

  • Layer 1: Engagement mechanics. XP systems, streaks, daily rewards, and quests drive habitual activity. Members who visit daily are 8x more likely to convert to paid tiers. We build the systems that create this habit loop.
  • Layer 2: Social proof and scarcity. Limited-edition roles, numbered badges, and exclusive channels create aspirational value. When members see others with premium perks, conversion rates increase. We design these mechanics into the server architecture.
  • Layer 3: Payment infrastructure. Stripe integration for subscriptions and one-time purchases. Automatic role assignment, renewal reminders, failed payment recovery, and churn prevention workflows. Zero friction between "I want this" and "I have this."
  • Layer 4: Retention and expansion. Loyalty rewards for long-term subscribers. Upgrade incentives when members approach tier limits. Win-back campaigns for churned members. The goal is not just acquisition but lifetime value maximization.
  • Layer 5: Analytics and optimization. Track which features drive conversions, which channels retain paying members, and where drop-off occurs. Data-driven iteration on pricing, perks, and positioning. We build the measurement layer so you can optimize continuously.
