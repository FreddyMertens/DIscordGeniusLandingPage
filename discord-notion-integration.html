Discord ↔ Notion automation features

Bridge your team's communication hub with your knowledge base.

Slash command → Notion page

Type /task in Discord and a new Notion database entry is created instantly — with title, assignee, priority, and due date pre-filled.

Database change notifications

When a Notion page status changes, a Discord message posts to the relevant channel. Keep everyone informed without manual pings.

Two-way sync

Update a task in Notion and it reflects in Discord. React to a Discord message and it updates the Notion record. Full bidirectional flow.

Notion search from Discord

Query your Notion workspace directly from Discord. Find docs, pages, and database entries without leaving the chat.

Meeting notes & standup logs

Bot auto-creates daily standup pages in Notion from Discord thread summaries. Meeting notes flow from voice channels to docs.

Ticket → Notion pipeline

Support tickets opened in Discord auto-create Notion entries with all context. Close the ticket and the Notion status updates too.

Popular Discord + Notion workflows

  • Bug tracking — /bug command creates a Notion bug report with severity, reproduction steps, and auto-assigned developer
  • Content calendar — Notion content calendar posts reminders to Discord when deadlines approach or statuses change
  • Knowledge base surfacing — Members ask a question in Discord, bot searches your Notion wiki and surfaces the relevant article
  • Applicant pipeline — Discord application forms create Notion entries; staff moves them through stages, and applicants get DM updates
  • Sprint boards — Notion sprint database syncs task assignments and progress to a dedicated Discord channel with daily summaries
