FAQ

What service integrations do you provide?

If your service has an API, we integrate it. If you run the service yourself, we’ll design the API and build the integration for you. Popular: Stripe, Whop, Shopify, Notion, Airtable, Zapier, Make, Slack, Intercom, HubSpot, Google Sheets, Twilio, Plaid.

Can you build novel, custom bots for unique workflows?

Yes. Slash commands, scheduled jobs, purchase-to-role, licensing, gated channels, dashboards, analytics events, and any bespoke workflow your community dreams up.

Do you support AI features in bots?

We can integrate OpenAI or on-device models for Q&A, summarization, moderation aids, and more, with strict guardrails and privacy controls.

How do you handle bot token security?

Tokens are stored in encrypted vaults (AWS Secrets Manager or similar), never logged or exposed. We follow Discord's security best practices and can provide security audit reports.

What access do you need to our server?

Minimum required permissions for the specific features we're building. We'll document exactly what we need and why. You maintain full admin control.

Do you handle full server design and branding?

Yes. Information architecture, channel taxonomy, visual identity, onboarding flows, and role systems tailored to your brand.

What UX improvements do you implement?

Clear wayfinding, progressive onboarding, contextual help, accessibility-minded copy, and frictionless action paths for members.

Do you provide in-house design for logos, banners, and bot UX?

Yes. Our in-house designer creates logos, banners, emoji/sticker packs, and designs custom UX flows for bot interactions (buttons, selects, modals) including copywriting.

Can you design emoji/stickers and other brand assets?

Absolutely. We craft cohesive emoji and sticker packs, channel artwork, and templates that reinforce your brand.

What community features can you build?

  • Quests, XP, streaks, leaderboards
  • VIP tiers, staff tools, application workflows
  • Events, reminders, attendance tracking

Do you provide ticketing/helpdesk?

Yes. Ticketing, SLAs, escalation rules, and knowledge base surfacing, integrated with Notion/Airtable if needed.

What analytics do you provide?

  • Engagement funnels, retention cohorts
  • Feature adoption, moderation KPIs
  • Revenue attribution, LTV/CAC, cohort profitability

Do you export or dashboard data?

CSV exports, privacy-safe event design, and dashboards tailored to your metrics.

How do you approach safety?

Role/permission hardening, rate limits, audit logs, incident response playbooks, and abuse mitigation automations.

Do you support compliance needs?

We support data minimization, retention policies, and secure secrets handling. Formal audits can be coordinated.

How do requests work day-to-day?

Queue unlimited requests in Trello. We deliver one at a time with Average 96-hour turnaround; larger items progress continuously.

What formats do you accept for briefs?

Trello cards, Google Docs, Loom videos(if it can be linked), it works.

What if I'm not satisfied with a delivery?

We iterate until you're 100% satisfied, unlimited revisions included. Most requests are approved after 1-2 revision cycles. If we fundamentally can't build what you need, we'll let you know upfront and help find alternatives.

How do you prioritize multiple requests?

First-in, first-out from your Trello queue. You can reorder anytime. Urgent bug fixes are prioritized automatically.

Are bots reliable at scale?

We design for resilience: retries, circuit breakers, background jobs, and monitoring with alerts.

Do you support staging environments?

Yes: safe rollout through dev/stage/prod, with feature flags where applicable.

What's included in $699/mo?

One active request at a time, unlimited queue, unlimited brands/servers, up to 2 users, pause/cancel anytime.

Can I pause my subscription?

Yes. Billing pauses with unused days banked for later use.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit card via Stripe. Invoicing available for annual plans.

What's NOT included?

3D modeling, video production, physical hardware, 24/7 emergency on-call (enterprise only), content moderation services (we build tools, you moderate).

Who owns the code and assets?

You do. All code, assets, bots, and IP are 100% yours upon delivery. We retain no rights and sign over full ownership.

Do you host and run everything for me?

Yes. We handle all hosting, monitoring, updates, and maintenance so you never have to think about infrastructure. Your bots stay online and secure 24/7.

Can I run everything myself instead?

Absolutely. We deliver clean, documented code you can self-host anytime. We'll provide deployment guides and assist with the handoff if you want to take over operations.

What if I want to migrate to another developer?

No problem. Everything we build is yours. We'll package up all code, documentation, and credentials, then help the new team get up to speed. Zero lock-in, clean handoff.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. We can sign your mutual NDA prior to onboarding.

What about support and fixes?

Bug fixes are prioritized. Support requests can be added to your queue anytime.

Do you offer SLAs?

We target ~96-hour average delivery. Formal SLAs can be discussed for enterprise needs.

How do I contact you for urgent issues?

Email hello@DiscordGenius.dev for urgent bugs. Response within 12 hours, critical fixes prioritized within 24 hours.
