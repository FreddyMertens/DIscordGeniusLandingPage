Verification features we build

Layer multiple verification methods to create the exact security posture your community needs.

CAPTCHA verification

Image-based, text-based, or button-click CAPTCHAs that stop automated bot accounts while keeping the experience frictionless for real users.

Phone verification

SMS or phone-number verification to ensure one account per person. Prevents alt accounts and mass-join raids.

Account-age gates

Require Discord accounts to be a minimum age before gaining access. Configurable thresholds with staff override capabilities.

Quiz onboarding

Members answer questions about your community rules or niche before gaining access. Ensures they've actually read the guidelines.

Anti-raid detection

Real-time monitoring for mass-join spikes, message floods, and suspicious patterns. Automatic lockdown with staff alerts.

Multi-step flows

Chain multiple verification steps: agree to rules, then CAPTCHA, then quiz, then role assignment. Fully customizable per-channel or per-role.

Why custom verification?

  • Generic bots are bypassable. Spammers know how to get around Dyno, MEE6, and Wick. Custom verification logic is unique and much harder to automate around.
  • Brand-matched experience. Your verification flow looks and feels like your community, not a generic bot prompt that screams "unmanaged server."
  • Integrated with your stack. Tie verification to your CRM, payment system, or application forms. Automatically route verified members to the right channels.
  • Audit logs and analytics. Track verification success rates, drop-off points, flagged accounts, and raid attempts with detailed dashboards.
