Discord ↔ Airtable automation features

Replace fragile Zapier chains with a purpose-built integration that does exactly what you need.

Record sync

Create, update, and delete Airtable records from Discord commands. Changes in Airtable post real-time updates back to Discord channels.

Form → record pipeline

Discord form submissions (applications, tickets, feedback) auto-create Airtable records with all fields mapped. No manual data entry.

Workflow triggers

When an Airtable record hits a specific status, trigger Discord actions: send DMs, assign roles, post announcements, or create threads.

Airtable search from Discord

Query your Airtable bases directly from Discord. Find records, filter by fields, and get formatted results without leaving chat.

Scheduled reports

Daily, weekly, or monthly summaries pulled from Airtable and posted to Discord. Pipeline status, KPIs, overdue tasks. All automated.

View-based notifications

Subscribe Discord channels to specific Airtable views. When records enter or leave a view, get instant notifications with context.
